The 99 Names of Allah (Asma ul-Husna): Complete Guide

By DeenPal Team· May 14, 2026· 11 min read

The 99 Names of Allah, Asma ul-Husna (the Beautiful Names), are the most powerful and meaningful words in Islam. They describe who Allah is: His mercy, His power, His justice, His wisdom, His love. Reciting them, understanding them, and living by them is one of the highest forms of worship.

"And to Allah belong the most beautiful names, so call upon Him by them."

Al-A'raf 7:180

The Promise of Paradise

The Prophet ﷺ said: "Indeed, Allah has 99 names, one hundred minus one. Whoever ahsaha will enter Paradise." (Bukhari & Muslim) The word ahsaha doesn't just mean "memorise", scholars explain it includes: knowing the names, understanding their meanings, calling on Allah by them in dua, and acting in accordance with what they imply.

How to Benefit from the Names

1. Memorise them gradually, Don't try to learn all 99 in one sitting. Memorise 3-5 per week, with their meanings.

2. Use them in dua, Allah said: "And to Allah belong the most beautiful names, so call upon Him by them." When you ask for mercy, call Him Ar-Rahman. When you ask for forgiveness, call Him Al-Ghaffar. When you ask for provision, call Him Ar-Razzaq.

3. Let them shape your character, Knowing Allah is Al-Aleem (All-Knowing) keeps you mindful of sin. Knowing He is At-Tawwab (Acceptor of Repentance) keeps you from despair. Knowing He is Al-Hakeem (All-Wise) helps you trust Him in hardship.
